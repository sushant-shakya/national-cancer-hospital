About Vision Mission

Welcome to National Hospital and Cancer Center, where the story begins with hope and ends with triumph. National Cancer Hospital (NCH) is committed to providing comprehensive cancer care. Being the sole cancer centre we have been serving with surgery, medicine, chemotherapy, Day Care, Hospice and other facilities. While our roots lie in cancer care, our commitment extends beyond boundaries. Amidst the complexities of this formidable disease, our committed, experienced and updated health professionals are confident in providing the best health solutions to our patients for their well-being. At the National Hospital & Cancer Center (NCH), our vision extends beyond medical treatments. We are committed to empowering patients throughout their cancer journey. Our personalized approach combines cutting-edge therapies with compassionate support, ensuring that each patient becomes an active participant in their healing process. Read More

Our mission at the National Hospital & Cancer Center (NCH) is to provide comprehensive and compassionate cancer care to every patient, regardless of their background or circumstances. We prioritize personalized attention, addressing physical, emotional, and social needs. Our commitment extends to Nepali patients, ensuring they receive world-class treatment right here in Nepal. Moreover, we adhere to the latest guidelines and strive to offer advanced medical treatments. Together, we create a community where cancer is not just battled—it’s conquered. Read More

About

National Cancer Hospital (NCH), established in 2009, stands as a guiding force in Nepal’s battle against cancer. Our unwavering mission is to provide accessible, top-tier care to patients nationwide. With a dedicated team of experienced doctors, we bring years of collective expertise to the forefront of cancer treatment.
