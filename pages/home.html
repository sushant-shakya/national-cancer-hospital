About Vision Mission

Welcome to National Hospital and Cancer Center, where the story begins with hope and ends with triumph. National Cancer Hospital (NCH) is committed to providing comprehensive cancer care. Being the sole cancer centre we have been serving with surgery, medicine, chemotherapy, Day Care, Hospice and other facilities. While our roots lie in cancer care, our commitment extends beyond boundaries.
