Welcome Message from the Chairman

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to National Hospital and Cancer Center. As the Chairman, I am honored to lead an institution committed to excellence in healthcare, compassion in service, and innovation in medical practice.

Our hospital stands as a beacon of hope, where skilled professionals work tirelessly to deliver the highest quality of care with empathy and respect. Whether you are a patient, a family member, or a well-wisher, we are here to support you every step of the way.

We believe in a holistic approach to healing — combining advanced medical technology with personalized attention to meet the unique needs of every individual. Your health, comfort, and trust are at the heart of everything we do.

About

National Cancer Hospital (NCH), established in 2009, stands as a guiding force in Nepal’s battle against cancer. Our unwavering mission is to provide accessible, top-tier care to patients nationwide. With a dedicated team of experienced doctors, we bring years of collective expertise to the forefront of cancer treatment.
